<b>Human-AI Interaction Researcher</b>

Hello! 👋 I'm a Computer Science PhD student at [Virginia Tech](https://www.vt.edu/)'s [Sanghani Center for Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics](https://sanghani.cs.vt.edu/about/), advised by Prof. [Chris North](https://people.cs.vt.edu/north/). Leveraging three years of industrial experience in full-stack and machine learning engineering, I design and implement human-AI collaborative systems using state-of-the-art LLM and agentic frameworks. I bridge the gap between AI reasoning and human oversight by architecting intuitive visual interfaces that prioritize system <b>transparency</b>, <b>generalizability</b>, and <b>operational efficiency</b>. My unique background in large-scale recommendation systems allows me to transition <b>abstract cognitive frameworks</b> into <b>robust, production-ready solutions</b>. 

<b> 👉Explore my latest system, [S-PRISM](https://s-prism.netlify.app/), to see human-AI collaboration in action. Simply click 'Report Generation' from the header, recognize the space, and re-run the generation to experience the system's responsiveness and transparency firsthand. </b>

Before joining Virginia Tech, I spent nearly three years in industry, starting as a full-stack development engineer before advancing to machine learning engineering where I worked on large-scale recommendation systems.
I hold both B.S. and M.S. degrees from [Wuhan University](https://en.whu.edu.cn/). Throughout my PhD and master's studies, I've had the privilege of collaborating with distinguished researchers from premier institutions including MIT, Georgia Tech, Tsinghua University, City University of Hong Kong, and Adobe Research, among others.
